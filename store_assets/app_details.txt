Full Description
- With the advent of technology, our young ones have also gotten involved in using various devices in the modern world. Not only was it a struggle for a parent to minimize their screen time but was also an effort to divert their attention to other productive things physically. This study aims to develop a task management module that implements a first in first out (FIFO) queue algorithm along with integrating gamification in the form of a virtual pet game system, and incentive system to stimulate engagement and motivation to children.
